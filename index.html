Expression through movement

Rebecca won 1st place and overall highest score for her division in the Summerdanse Competition 2015 in Rochester, NY. When she was only two years old, and watching her sister in an opera, she said: "I want to dance on the stage like Rachel."



Rebecca has dreamed of dancing all her life. She's been taking ballet lessons for 11 years and she has been accepted to the incredibly competitive and prestigious Nutmeg Ballet Conservatory 2018 Summer Intensive. She is one of only 140 ballet students accepted every year from 25 States in the U.S. Her family can't afford the steep tuition for this program. If you're able to donate to this fund, you would be helping to make her dream come true.
